Pizza recipe

How to make a pizza

When we make a pizza we usually ask ourselves should I buy or make the dough? Granted, making the dought requires more time and effort as opposed to simply buying it from your local store. However, there is some art in making your own meals from time to time. We have to take certain factors into consideration: yeast, flour, sauce and meats.

Ingredients: for the pizza dough

  • All-purpose flour or bread flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Sugar

Ingredients: for the pizza sauce

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the baking sheet and/or pan)
  • Tomato Sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Feta cheese
  • Some shrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Italian pepperoncinin
  • Italian sausages
  • Sliced black olives
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Baby arugula
  • Pesto
  • Pepperoni
  • Onions
  • ham

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast

  2. Make and knead the pizza dough

  3. Preheat the baking sheet

  4. Divide the dough into toppings

  5. Prep the toppings

  6. Flatten the dough ball and stretch it out into a round

  7. Brush the dough top with olive oil

  8. Sprinkle the pizza dough with cornmeal and flour

  9. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings

  10. Slide pizza into oven

  11. Bake pizza in the 475Â°F oven, one at a time, until the crust is browned and the cheese is golden, about 10 to 15 minutes.
