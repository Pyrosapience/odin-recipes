Lemon Pie recipe

How to make a lemon Pie

There's no real summer break without some pie! Without some lemon pie that is. Today we're gonna see the behind the scenes and cast of a true lemon pie. Let's hope y'all haven't forgotten about the good ol' meringue that goes a long way back with the pie. A lemon pie without meringue is like a Romeo without his Juliet.

Ingredients: for pie

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of water
  • 3 large beaten egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of grated lemon zest
  • 1 baked pastry shell

Ingredients: for Meringue

  • 3 large egg whites - room temperature
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, salt, cornstarch, and flour. Gradually stir in water. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat; cook and stir 2 minutes more. Remove from the heat. Gradually stir 1 cup of hot mixture into egg yolks; return all to saucepan. Bring to boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. Stir in the butter, lemon juice and zest until smooth. Pour into pastry shell.

  2. In a bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff (but not dry) peaks form. Gradually beat in sugar until soft peaks form. Spread over pie, sealing edges to pastry. Bake at 350Â° until meringue is golden, 12-15 minutes. Cool. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator.
